Have an idea?

For general inquiries, please email us at info@tedxauckland.com

TEDxAuckland is an idea-sharing platform set on sharing innovations with a global audience. If you know someone who belongs on the TEDxAuckland stage, then we want to hear from you!

Please present your idea or nominate a speaker by emailing ideas@tedxauckland.com

We ask that you be clear on the idea and enlighten us on how it will inspire individuals and have a positive impact on our world. TEDxAuckland is not a storytelling platform, which means we are most interested in hearing your idea.
